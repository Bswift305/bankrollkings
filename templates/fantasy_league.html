Player Rankings & Projections
{{ projection_total }} Players
Proj is simulation-driven: we simulate 2,000 games per player by resampling their real logged games weighted toward recent form — drawing whole games keeps stats correlated the way they are in real life (big minutes lift points, rebounds, and assists together). Ceiling/Floor are the 90th/10th percentile of those simulations (a good night vs a bad night, not all-time extremes), and Boom% is how often the sims land 20%+ above projection. Fantasy points follow the scoring style you pick — DraftKings (incl. double-double bonuses), FanDuel, or Yahoo. Avg/L5 are the raw season and last-5 baselines; Trend is L5 minus Avg. The sims also react to tonight's situation: the live injury report (teammate out boosts a player's simulated games, a star returning suppresses them — the ±% chip next to a name, hover for the reason) and the matchup (when a player's team is on the odds board, Opp shows the opponent and whether that defense gives up more or less fantasy production than average — Soft / Neutral / Tough, shifting the sims a few %). All shifts combined are capped at ±15% so situation noise can't swamp real form. Columns marked SOON land with the slate data feed: salaries, value (FP per $1K), and projected ownership.
