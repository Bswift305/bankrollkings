Market Read
What is the number saying?
Start with spread, total, price shape, and book context. The market is part of the handicap, not just the price tag.
Matchup Read
How does the game want to play?
Use environment, style, and team behavior to understand whether the spot supports a side, a total, or no bet at all.
Trend Read
Does the history actually support it?
Streaks and continuation patterns only matter if they survive review. This layer is here to confirm or challenge the first read.
Plain-English Verdict
Clean support or conflict?
The goal is not to overwhelm you with variables. The goal is to show whether the signals agree, partially agree, or should keep you off the bet.
