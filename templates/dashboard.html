Platform Lens
Open Market Edge
Market Read
Where the number moved
Use open-to-now movement, book splits, and price disagreement before trusting any prop row.
Example: Opened 12.5, now 13.5. The over got more expensive, so the early number was the better one.
Matchup Read
How tonight should play
This board exists to turn team-vs-defense context into a cleaner read on game script and angle fit.
Example: If the opponent gives up a lot of guard threes, a shooting guard over may deserve more trust than usual.
Trend Read
Short sample or real support?
Trend support only matters when the line history and current-team sample are not fighting the play.
Example: 4 of the last 5 only matters if the player was in the same role and playing similar minutes.
Verdict
Attack, support, or avoid
The goal is not to flood the card with plays. It is to understand which spots actually deserve action.
Example: Attack = clean edge, Support = usable but fragile, Avoid = too many conflicts.
