Product Definition

Reserved for real conditional tendencies

A streak says a player has hit several times in a row. A tendency says the hit rate changes when a condition is true: home, rest, favorite status, total band, opponent type, or official/umpire context.

Current policy: Streak logic stays in Streak Lab. Tendency logic must be conditional hit-rate logic. Officiating profiles should surface here and on sport dashboards when assignments are live.
